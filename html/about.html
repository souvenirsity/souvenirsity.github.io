Our Brand

Souvenirsity started as our project. We wanted to show the world the power of e-commerce and e-marketing. Through this website we have, we showcase the different kinds of interactions a user can get through online shopping. The products we have shown here are 100% Filipino-made as we support Filipino artists in this platform that we have. We hope to have more products soon as we expand the scope of our services.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by University of the East students, we wanted to fill the void of unequal merchandise being given to schools. Some schools have more while some schools have less. With that in mind, we created this website equally without bias to any particular school. All 8 Universities have products in each category as we believe that in equality, there is unity.
